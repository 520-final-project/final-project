Our Mission
Why We Built AboutOcean

AboutOcean was born from an urgent belief: Earth is warning us, and education is the most powerful response. As our final-year project, we use interactive digital experiences to bring the spirit of two UN SDGs to every user.

- SDG 12 Responsible Consumption and Production
The world produces 2 billion tons of waste yearly, but less than 20% is recycled. One-third of food is wasted of food is wasted, driving emissions. SDG 12 calls us to rethink consumption—cutting plastic and buying local—to shape our planet's future.

- SDG 14 Life Below Water
The ocean covers 71% of Earth, supporting 230,000 species and 3 billion people. However, a truckload of plastic is dumped every minute, and 1/3 of fisheries are overfished. SDG 14 aims to protect 30% of the ocean by 2030 and end pollution and harmful subsidies.

Protecting the ocean starts with our daily choices. We believe this generation can make real change happen.

The SDGs We Champion
12
Responsible Consumption & Production

SDG 12 calls on all nations and individuals to reshape how goods are produced and consumed — reducing waste, increasing efficiency, and decoupling economic growth from environmental destruction.

Halve per-capita global food waste by 2030
Achieve sustainable management of natural resources
Substantially reduce waste generation through prevention & recycling
14
Life Below Water

SDG 14 aims to conserve and sustainably use the oceans, seas, and marine resources. This includes tackling plastic pollution, ending harmful fishing subsidies, and protecting at least 10% of coastal and marine areas.

Prevent and reduce marine pollution of all kinds
Protect at least 30% of global ocean by 2030
End overfishing and destructive fishing practices
